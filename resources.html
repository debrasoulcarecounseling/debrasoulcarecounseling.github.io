Patient Resources

Explore a variety of resources to support your mental health journey. Below you will find links to trusted websites and a plethora of information to help you on your path to well-being.

Mental Health Information

National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH): The lead federal agency for research on mental disorders. Provides information on a variety of mental health topics.

MentalHealth.gov: One-stop access to U.S. government mental health information.

World Health Organization (WHO) Mental Health: Information and resources on global mental health issues.

Support and Crisis Helplines

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a Crisis Counselor.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: Call 1-800-273-8255 for 24/7 support.

Samaritans: Provides emotional support to anyone in distress, struggling to cope, or at risk of suicide.

Therapy and Counseling Services

Psychology Today: Find a therapist or psychiatrist in your area.

BetterHelp: Online therapy from licensed professionals.

Talkspace: Online therapy and counseling from licensed therapists.

Self-Help and Wellness

Headspace: Guided meditation and mindfulness resources.

Mindful: Resources for mindfulness practices and improving mental well-being.

SELF: Articles and resources on wellness, fitness, and self-care.

Resources for Veterans

VA Mental Health: Resources and support for veterans' mental health provided by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs.

National Center for PTSD: Information on PTSD and resources for treatment and support for veterans.

Wounded Warrior Project: Support and programs for veterans and service members who have been injured.

Resources for Students

Active Minds: Empowering students to speak openly about mental health in order to educate others and encourage help-seeking.

JED Foundation: Protecting emotional health and preventing suicide for teens and young adults.

NAMI Teens & Young Adults: Information and resources for young people dealing with mental health issues.

Resources for Parents

Parenting Mental Health: Support for parents of children with mental health issues.

Understood: Resources for parents of children with learning and attention issues.

Zero to Three: Resources for parents of infants and toddlers to support early development and mental health.

Resources for LGBTQ+ Individuals

The Trevor Project: Crisis intervention and suicide prevention services for LGBTQ+ youth.

LGBT National Help Center: Free and confidential support for LGBTQ+ individuals.

GLAAD Resource List: A comprehensive list of resources and support for the LGBTQ+ community.
