A Little About Me

Welcome to Debra Soul Care Counseling. I am passionate about providing compassionate and effective mental health support to help you navigate life's challenges.

My Story

My journey into counseling began with a deep desire to help others find peace and healing. With over 15 years of experience in the field, I have dedicated my career to supporting individuals through their mental health journeys. My approach is integrative, combining evidence-based practices with a holistic perspective to address the unique needs of each client.

I believe in the power of a strong therapeutic relationship and strive to create a safe, non-judgmental space where clients can explore their thoughts and feelings. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or just need someone to talk to, I am here to help you find your way.

My Approach

My counseling approach is client-centered and collaborative. I utilize a variety of therapeutic techniques, including cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based stress reduction (MBSR), and solution-focused brief therapy (SFBT), tailored to meet the individual needs of my clients.

I am committed to ongoing professional development and staying current with the latest research and best practices in the field of mental health. My goal is to empower you with the tools and insights needed to overcome challenges and build a fulfilling, balanced life.
